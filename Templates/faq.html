Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I place an order?

Simply browse through our products, click on your favorite, select the quantity, and hit the 'Add to Cart' or 'Buy Now' button.

How long does delivery take?

Typically, orders are delivered within 5-7 working days nationwide.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free nationwide delivery on all orders above Rs. 750.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) only. Online payment options are coming soon.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes, products can be returned within 7 days in their original, unused condition. See our Refund Policy for more details.
